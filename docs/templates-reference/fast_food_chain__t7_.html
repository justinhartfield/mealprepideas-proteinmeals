How to Hack
the Menu.

01

The "Green Sub"

Swap any bread-based side (chips/baguette) for a tomato cucumber salad or an apple. Saves 150 cals, adds fiber.

02

Double-Down Protein

Panera allows "Extra Meat" on almost all digital orders. It’s the highest ROI way to hit a 50g+ protein target.

03

Dressing Decoupling

Always order dressing "On the Side". Most salad dressings at Panera contain 15g-22g of fat per serving.

Target: P:E Ratio

The Protein-to-Energy (P:E) ratio is our gold standard. At Panera, aim for a ratio of 1.0 or higher (grams of protein vs fat+carbs).

Protocol FAQ

What is the highest protein item at Panera?

The Toasted Steak & White Cheddar (whole) leads with 48g of protein, but it also carries 840 calories. For better density, the Green Goddess Chicken Salad (double chicken) is the winner.

Are Panera's warm bowls healthy?

They are nutrient-dense but can be high in sodium and carbohydrates. The Mediterranean Bowl is the most "system-friendly" due to its high fiber count (12g).
