Meal Prep Logistics

  • Storage: Use airtight glass containers. Lasts 5 days in refrigeration (40°F / 4°C).

  • Batching: Double the recipe to hit 8 portions. Use two sheet pans to avoid steaming the vegetables.

  • Reheat: Microwave for 90s at 70% power to prevent chicken from drying out.

Execution Steps

01
The Macro Prep

Dice chicken into uniform 1-inch cubes. Pat dry with paper towels to ensure browning. Toss with lemon juice, zest, and half the garlic.

02
High Heat Sear

Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium-high. Add chicken in a single layer. Do not crowd. Cook for 4-5 mins until internal temp hits 165°F.

03
Veggie Steam-Sauté

Remove chicken. Add broccoli and 2 tbsp water. Cover for 2 mins to steam, then remove lid, add remaining garlic, and sauté until tender-crisp.

04
The Assembly

Divide quinoa into 4 containers. Top with chicken and broccoli. Garnish with fresh parsley and an extra squeeze of lemon.
