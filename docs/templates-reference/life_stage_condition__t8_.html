Clinical Advisory

This protocol optimizes for recovery. Always consult your obstetrician or midwife before introducing new supplements or radical calorie changes during breastfeeding.

Focus: Anti-Inflammatory

Safe Defaults

Optimize These
  • Bone-broth based soups (Mineral rich)
  • Slow-cooked meats (Easy digestion)
  • Overnight oats with brewers yeast
  • Roasted root vegetables (Complex carbs)
  • Salmon & Omega-3 rich fats

"Warm, soft, and easy to digest" is the golden rule for the first 2 weeks.

Limit / Avoid

Reduce Load
  • Iced drinks & raw salads (First 10 days)
  • Excessive caffeine (Lactation interference)
  • Highly processed seed oils
  • Gas-heavy cruciferous (If baby is sensitive)
  • Empty sugar calories (Energy crashes)

Focus on "nourishing from the inside out" to minimize cortisol spikes.
