THE 90-MINUTE
SUNDAY EXECUTION

01
T-Minus 90m

Oven & Grain Prep

Preheat oven to 425°F. Chop sweet potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes. Roast with olive oil and salt. Start the lentils in the steamer if not pre-steamed.

02
T-Minus 60m

Protein Batching

Brown the ground turkey in a large skillet. Season with taco spices or basic garlic/salt. In a second pan, sear chicken breast chunks. Slice tofu into squares.

03
T-Minus 15m

Assembly Matrix

Portion out cauliflower rice into 10 containers. Top with turkey or chicken. Add roasted potatoes. Seal and label with macro markers.

Storage & Reheating Guide

Keep Turkey Bowls in the fridge for up to 4 days. Freeze days 5-7. Reheat at 60% power for 2 minutes to keep the ground turkey moist. Yogurt bowls should be assembled fresh daily to prevent "grit" texture from flax seeds.
