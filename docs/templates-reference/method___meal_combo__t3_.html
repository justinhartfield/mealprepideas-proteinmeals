SYSTEM FAQ

Can I cook eggs in a crockpot overnight?

Yes, but only as part of a casserole with structural components like potatoes or bread. Plain eggs will rubberize. Use the 'Low' setting and ensure the pot is at least half-full for even heat distribution.

Are oats better in a slow cooker?

Steel-cut oats excel in the crockpot. Unlike rolled oats, they maintain a "pop" and creamy texture over 8 hours that microwave methods cannot replicate.

How long do these meals last in the fridge?

Our protocol standard is 4 days (Monday-Thursday) to maintain peak moisture levels and food safety standards for high-protein poultry/eggs.
