Storage Architecture.

01

The Flat-Freeze Method

Freeze soups and curries in Ziploc bags laid flat. This maximizes freezer space and reduces thawing time by 70%.

02

The "Eat Cold" Safety Net

Always include 20% handheld, cold-tolerant protein (like turkey meatballs) for the moments when even the microwave is too much work.

03

Tactical Labeling

Label by "Reheat Instruction" rather than just name. E.g., "Melt on High - 4 mins" instead of "Dhal".
