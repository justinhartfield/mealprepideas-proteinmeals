High-Protein Diet Vegan

VEGAN
PROTEIN.

Functional Definition

High-protein veganism focuses on maximizing the Protein-to-Energy (P:E) ratio using plant-exclusive sources. This strategy prioritizes legumes, soy isolates, and vital wheat gluten to achieve muscle-sparing results without the caloric density of traditional plant-based "carbohydrate-heavy" diets.

Best for aggressive fat loss while preserving lean mass.
Optimized for logistics (batch-prep soy & seitan).
