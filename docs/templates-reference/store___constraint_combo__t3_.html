Protocol FAQ

What makes a food "Low Calorie" in this system?

We define low calorie as any whole food or prepared item that stays under 150 calories per standard serving, allowing for a 3-component meal to stay under 500 calories total.

Is Aldi protein actually higher quality than competitors?

Aldi's core protein (beef, salmon, tuna) meets the same USDA standards as major retailers. The value lies in their lean SKU availability like Grass-Fed 90/10 beef at a lower price point.

Are these prices accurate?

Prices are based on mid-2024 regional averages and will fluctuate by location. We update these SKUs monthly based on stock availability.
