1

Thermal Calibration

Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a heavy-duty sheet pan with parchment paper. Efficiency note: Do not use foil if planning to reheat in microwave later to avoid stripping into the food.

2

Aromatic Infusion

In a large bowl, toss the cubed chicken with olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, and herbs. Let sit for 10 minutes while the oven reaches temperature.

3

Systemic Layout

Spread chicken in a single layer. Ensure pieces are not touching to allow for proper airflow and browning rather than steaming. Place lemon slices on top to keep chicken moist during high-heat blast.

4

The Batch Bake

Roast for 18-22 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Remove and let rest for 5 minutes before portioning into glass containers.

Cooling Protocol

Do not seal lids until food is cool to the touch. Trapped steam creates a "soggy chicken" texture upon reheating.

The Reheat Fix

Microwave for 90 seconds at 70% power. This preserves the moisture in the lean breast meat better than 100% power.

Storage Limit

System-stable for 5 days in airtight glass. For 7+ days, freeze immediately after cooling in freezer-safe bags.
